About Me

My name is John Oliyide, and I’ve always been driven by two things: technology and creativity. I started out building websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and over time I’ve grown into a developer who cares deeply about clean design, responsive layouts, and making digital experiences feel effortless.

Today, I’m proud to be part of Apple, where I’m sharpening my skills in network security and working toward building a long-term career in tech innovation. I enjoy tackling challenges that push me to learn and grow, and I see every project as a chance to raise the bar.

Outside of coding, I’m a musician at heart. I play the drums and founded Johannu Records International, a record label in Nigeria that gives upcoming artists a platform to share their voices. Music keeps me grounded and reminds me that creativity comes in many forms.

Whether it’s writing code, producing music, or exploring new ideas in security, I bring passion, focus, and a drive to keep improving. This portfolio is just one way I’m sharing my journey — and I’m excited about where it’s headed next.
